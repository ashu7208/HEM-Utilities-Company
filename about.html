Company Overview

Multi-domain expertise in Electricity, Water and Environment

HEM Utilities Pvt. Ltd is a Knowledge Company having multi-domain expertise in the Electricity, Water and Environment sectors. Our agenda is to contribute to the adoption of best practices, technologies and innovative ideas for performance improvement across Industries, Utilities and Government Bodies.

We have developed a robust mechanism and ecosystem to provide products, solutions and services to Utilities, Industries, Residential-Commercial-Industrial facilities, Institutions, Consultants, Contractors, Manufacturers, Suppliers, Regulators, Policy Makers and Government Departments — supporting them in meeting their business objectives.

Founder

Mr. Pratap Dhumale

End-to-end Electricity Business Leadership

Mr. Pratap Dhumale, the founder of HEM Utilities, brings in end-to-end Electricity Business perspectives. His experience in the Power Sector spans Technical, Techno-Commercial, Regulatory, Business Process & Distribution Automation, SMART Metering & AMI, IT roll-out, Infrastructure and Business Development spheres.

Throughout his career, he has consistently taken on new challenges in planning and implementing new ideas, technologies and Government programs — characteristic of Corporate Entrepreneurship. His active contribution to revolutionary measures like SMART Grid Concept road-mapping, Electricity Act 2003 enforcement and R-APDRP implementation has delivered real benefits to end beneficiaries.

The core objective behind building the HEM Utilities framework is to enable success — technical, techno-commercial and business — for stakeholders across the Utility & Environment sector.
