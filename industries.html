Electricity

Electricity generation, transmission, distribution, supply and consumption are the core activities of the Electricity Sector. A major part of the sector remains under direct or indirect control of Governments in India.

Industry 02

Water

Reliable water distribution and quality assurance is foundational to public health, urban development and industrial growth. HEM Utilities applies its automation and infrastructure expertise to help utilities manage water as rigorously as they manage power.

  • Water Distribution Management Systems (WDMS) for network visibility
  • Water Quality Monitoring Systems and software
  • Electrical and Water Infrastructure EPC works
  • GIS-based asset mapping for distribution networks
Industry 03

Environment

According to the OECD, the environmental industry consists of activities which produce goods and services to measure, prevent, limit, minimise or correct environmental damage to water, air and soil — as well as problems related to waste, noise and eco-systems.

This sector deals with waste management, air pollution, water and waste, along with environmental services and equipment that reduce the ecological footprint of industrial and urban activity.
